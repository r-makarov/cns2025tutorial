with Jaxley and DendroTweaks

CNS Conference

View Schedule

About the Workshop

Detailed biophysical models have been instrumental for in-depth mechanistic understanding of neuronal activity and function. However, building biophysical models is challenging as their numerous free parameters make them hard to interpret, slow to simulate, and difficult to validate against experimental data.

In this tutorial, we will showcase methods for making biophysical models more intuitive and computationally efficient, using two new software tools: the Jaxley simulator and the DendroTweaks web application.
